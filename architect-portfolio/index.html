About

Designing with
purpose & precision

I'm Ar. Surya Sundaram — an architect and interior designer based in Chennai, India. I believe spaces should tell a story: one shaped by the people who inhabit them, the light that filters through them, and the materials that define them.

Collaborating with studios like Vibrant Spaces and Prism Innovations, I have worked across facade design, premium residentials and commercial interiors — always leading with empathy for the end user and rigour in execution.
