Vedha, Clover & Capella Residences

Chennai & Bangalore · Modern Rustic · Monochrome

Project Overview

A series of premium residential interior projects crafted with curated material palettes and bespoke furniture selections across two cities. Each residence was approached as a distinct narrative, shaped by the clients' personalities and the specific character of their spaces.

The Capella Residence embraces modern rustic decor — warmth of natural accents meets contemporary refinement in a fancy yet chill living space. Handpicked textures, earthy tones, and layered lighting create a home that feels both curated and effortlessly comfortable.

The Catheyst kitchen uses shades of cream in panels and cabinets to create a monochrome yet spacious feel, while Clover showcases layered textured wallpapers in a cocoon-like bedroom environment — intimate, cosy, and quietly luxurious.
