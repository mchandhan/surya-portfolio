Ullagaram Duplex House

Chennai, Tamil Nadu · Industrial Chic · Vibrant Spaces

Project Overview

The client wished for an elevation that was as uber-chic and effortlessly cool as they were. After a series of discovery sessions, we settled on an industrial design aesthetic — because nothing says personality quite like exposed brick walls and deconstructed décor.

The design plays with honest materials: raw concrete surfaces, tactile exposed brick, and dark metal accents. Bold geometric window articulations on the facade create a strong street presence while maintaining a warmth that feels genuinely lived-in rather than sterile.

The duplex configuration allowed for a clear vertical separation between public and private realms — the ground floor projects confidence while the upper level retreats into a more intimate, residential idiom, softened by recessed glazing and subtle shadow play.
