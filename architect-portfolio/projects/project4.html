30×40 Residence — Tumkur

Mahalakshmipuram, Tumkur, KA · 1,200 sq ft · Prism Innovations

Project Overview

A compact 30×40 site in Tumkur — where every square foot carries weight. The project involved complete 2D floor planning and 3D facade visualisation for the client, Mr. Sadashiva, developed under the studio Prism Innovations.

Design decisions prioritised natural light, efficient circulation, and a contemporary elevation that suits the suburban Tumkur context without appearing out of place. The facade reads as modern yet grounded — clean horizontal banding, considered proportions, and a restrained material palette.

From initial concept sketches through to final photorealistic renders and client approval, the process was collaborative and iterative — ensuring the design was both technically resolved and personally meaningful to the client.
