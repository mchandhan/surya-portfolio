Commercial Interior · Space Planning

Glo Clinic & Janata Seva HQ

Vijayanagara, Bangalore · 7,950 sq ft · Commercial

Project Overview

Two distinct commercial projects, united by a commitment to functional space planning that maximises efficiency while eliminating dead zones and awkward circulation.

For Glo Clinic, the brief was surgical: add a treatment room within the existing footprint and eliminate the wasted space around the generator area. Smart partition design and a rethought entry sequence achieved both goals without compromising workflow — a clean, clinical aesthetic that instils confidence in visitors.

The Janata Seva Co-operative Bank Headquarters project was a larger undertaking — full sanction drawings, complete 2D floor plans, and photorealistic 3D visualisations for a 7,950 sq ft commercial building. The design balances the gravity of a financial institution with a modern, approachable environment.
